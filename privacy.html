Privacy Policy :

This Privacy Policy outlines the methods by which we collect, use,protect, and disclose your information when your engage with our software and related services. By using our software, you agree to the practices described herein.

1.Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users, regardless of their location. It governs the information collected through:
  • The software application.
  • Related websites,portals, or support platforms.
  • Any communication with our customer service team.

2.Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Informartion
We may collect personal data that identifies or relates to you, including but not limited to:
  • Full name,email address,phone number, and company information.
  • Login credentials such as usernames and encrypted passwords.
  • Payment information(processed securely via third-party providers.)
2.2 Non-Personal Information
Non-personal information is collected automatically and includes:
  • Device identifiers, operating system details, and browser type.
  • Internet Protocol(IP) address and gelocation data(where permitted).
  • Log files detailing usage patterns, such as pages accessed and session durations.
2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies, beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your interaction with our software to:
  • Personalize user experiences.
  • Track software performance and usage metrics.
  • Serve relevant advertisements(where applicable).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Trusted Service Providers
We work with third-party partners to provide specific services, such as:
  • Payment processing and fraud prevention.
  • Data hosting and cloud storage.
  • Analytics tools for understanding user behavior.
3.2 Compliance with Legal Obligations
We may disclose information to government authorities or law enforcement if required to:
  • Respond to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, employees, or the public.
3.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business, user information may be transferred to the successor entity under strict confidentiality agreements.
3.4 Aggregated and Anonymized Data
We may share anonymized datasets that cannot identify individuals for research, marketing, or bench marking purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data, including:
  • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest.
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and multifactor authentication.
  • While we strive to ensure absolute security, no system is entirely impenetrable. We encourage users to safeguard their credentials and report any suspicious activity.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your data, including:
5.1 Right to Access
You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
5.2 Right to Correction
You may correct inaccuracies in your personal data by contacting us.
5.3 Right to Deletion
You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
5.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You can restrict how we use your data under specific circumstances.
5.5 Right to Data Portability
Where applicable, you can request a copy of your data in a commonly used format.
5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
You may withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. International Data Transfers

If you access our software from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws. All international transfers are conducted in accordance with applicable regulations, such as Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our software is not designed for individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or business practices. Updates will be communicated via email or in-app notifications where applicable.
