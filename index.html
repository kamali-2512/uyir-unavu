Welcome to Uyirunavu



Uyirunavu is an initiative of Seva Trust aims to ensure food for the uncared elderly farmers and other dependents in rural areas of Tamil Nadu. Through our dedicated efforts, we reach out to the most vulnerable in society, ensuring they receive not just meals but also the love and care they deserve. Our initiative is a bridge of compassion, connecting generous hearts to those in need. Every meal served is a step towards a stronger, more inclusive community where everyone is valued. Join us in our journey to restore hope and provide a dignified life to our cherished elders.

Our Vision


We envision a society where rural elders receive the care and support they need to thrive, surrounded by their loved ones and familiar environments. We strive to bridge the gap in healthcare and social services, ensuring equal access to resources and opportunities for rural seniors.

Why Choosing Us!

Few Reasons Why Choosing Us!


We offer a holistic approach to elder care, ensuring their well-being at every step. Our healthcare assistance provides timely medical support, while our social support fosters meaningful connections. We prioritize nutrition and wellness, delivering balanced meals tailored to individual needs. With our transportation services, seniors enjoy safe, convenient travel to appointments and social activities. Our dedicated home care team ensures a comfortable and dignified living environment, allowing elders to age gracefully in their own homes.
