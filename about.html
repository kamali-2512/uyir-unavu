Welcome to Uyirunavu


Uyirunavu is an initiative of Seva Trust aims to ensure food for the uncared elderly farmers and other dependents in rural areas of Tamil Nadu. Through our dedicated efforts, we reach out to the most vulnerable in society, ensuring they receive not just meals but also the love and care they deserve. Our initiative is a bridge of compassion, connecting generous hearts to those in need. Every meal served is a step towards a stronger, more inclusive community where everyone is valued. Join us in our journey to restore hope and provide a dignified life to our cherished elders.

Our Vision


We envision a society where rural elders receive the care and support they need to thrive, surrounded by their loved ones and familiar environments. We strive to bridge the gap in healthcare and social services, ensuring equal access to resources and opportunities for rural seniors.

Our Values

Uyirunavu has been providing food everyday since January 2024 through a home delivery system to elderly farmers and it is already addressing some of the challenges that the rural elderly farmers are going through

Compassion
We care deeply about the well-being of rural elders.

Respect
We value the dignity and autonomy of each individual.

Empowerment
We enable seniors to make informed choices and live independently.

Community
We foster connections and support networks within rural communities.




Need of Uyirunavu

Why we deliver food for

elder


Mobility issues

Elderly farmers face challenges in traveling to markets or cooking meals due to physical limitations and health conditions


Limited access to nutritious food

Rural areas often have limited access to healthy food options, making it difficult for elderly farmers to maintain a balanced diet


Social isolation

Home delivery of food can help combat loneliness and social isolation, common among elderly farmers living


Improved health and well-being

Nutritious food delivery can improve elderly farmers' overall health, reducing malnutrition and related health issues.


Relief from cooking responsibilities

Home-delivered food alleviates the burden of cooking, allowing elderly farmers to focus on their well-being


Government support

Governments supports elderly farmers through food delivery initiatives, ensuring their well-being to agriculture.


Community engagement

Food delivery programs fosters community engagement, encouraging volunteers to interact with elderly farmers and build relationships.


Dignity and respect

Home-delivered food restores dignity and respect for elderly farmers, acknowledging their contributions to society
